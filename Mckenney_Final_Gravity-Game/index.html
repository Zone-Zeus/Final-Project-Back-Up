What Goes Up...?

Gravity, What Does It Do?

No matter where you go on Earth, something will always be holding you down. Every time you try to jump or throw a ball, it will always find its way back to the ground. Why is this you may ask? Well, every object in the universe has something called a gravitational pull and it is caused by the mass of the object. You may not be able to see how big the Earth really is but and it just so happens that Earth is of significant size and it weighs in at 6,000,000, 000,000,000,000,000,000 kilograms. Get the picture? 

How to Play?

This will be a simple and straightforward game to teach and show you how gravity works. You will be placed into a variety of scenarios in which you must choose the right object or position in which an object will land. Good luck.
